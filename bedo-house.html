Bedő House

History and Description

Emil Vidor was the architect commissioned to design the house. He had previously studied the architectural trends of the time in various Western European capitals, and had even worked with Miklós Ybl in designing Saint Stephen’s Basilica, the Opera House and a number of other high-profile buildings in Budapest. With such extensive experience under his belt, it is hardly surprising that both the interior and the exterior of the Bedő House display characteristic elements of Art Nouveau, such as natural motifs and asymmetrical forms.
